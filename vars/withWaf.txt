<p>
Provide the waf build tool.
</p>

<p>
Intended usage:
<pre>
	withWaf(gerrit_changes: 'x') {
		sish "waf setup --project some_project"
	}
</pre>
</p>

<p>
The (customized) waf build tool is available within the closure in shell scripts as 'waf'. Specific gerrit options for waf can be specified.
</p>
